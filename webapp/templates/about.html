About Kerala Archives

About

Kerala Archives is the official repository of records of historical value created and received by government and public institutions in the state of Kerala.

Our mission is to preserve, protect and provide access to archival records for research, education and administration. The Research Portal opens this collection to scholars and the public, with tools for searching documents, browsing visual galleries, and applying modern OCR and transliteration techniques to manuscripts in endangered scripts.

Purpose of the portal

  • Centralised, login-protected access to digitised archives.
  • Keyword search across titles, descriptions and tags.
  • Project workspaces — including the Malayanma palm-leaf transliteration tool.
  • A growing visual gallery of archive holdings and digitisation work.
